Anti‑Human Trafficking Policy

We have a zero tolerance policy for human trafficking, exploitation, or any non‑consensual content. Breyya does not knowingly host content involving trafficking, coercion, or minors.

All content published on this site is produced by, or with the informed consent of, verified adults. Performers are verified as 18 years of age or older with government‑issued photo identification on file.

If you suspect human trafficking, exploitation, or non‑consensual material, please report immediately to support@breyya.com or complaints@breyya.com. We will cooperate fully with law enforcement and take prompt action to remove and investigate any reported content.

For urgent reports, contact local law enforcement and notify us at complaints@breyya.com
