Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Breyya ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use breyya.com (the "Site").

We use the data we collect to provide and operate the Site, process subscriptions, communicate with you, and enhance user experience. We may also use aggregated or anonymized data for analytics and site improvement.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share data with service providers who perform services on our behalf (for example, CCBill for payment processing, hosting providers, and analytics services) and only to the extent necessary to provide those services.

5. Payment processing

All payments and billing are handled by CCBill. CCBill is responsible for securely processing and storing payment card information in a PCI-compliant manner. We do not store your full payment card details.

6. Data retention and deletion

We retain account information and related data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting support@breyya.com. Upon verified request we will remove personal data from our systems, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain records.

7. DMCA and copyright

If you believe content on the Site infringes your copyright, please contact us at support@breyya.com with a DMCA notice. We will respond and take action in accordance with applicable law.

8. Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To make a request, contact support@breyya.com. We may ask for information to verify your identity before processing requests.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or wish to request deletion of your data, contact us at support@breyya.com.
